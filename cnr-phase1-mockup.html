What's included in every rental

Every rental includes basic insurance with a $1,000 deductible, unlimited mileage within British Columbia, and 24/7 roadside assistance. Additional drivers can be added at the counter at no charge. Child seats, GPS units, and toll transponders are available on request — mention them in the booking notes and we'll have them ready at pickup.

Fuel policy is "same as pickup" — we hand the car over with a full tank and ask that you return it with a full tank. If you'd rather not, we'll refill it for you at cost plus a small service fee. Cleaning is on us for normal use; a detailing fee applies if the car comes back requiring more than a standard wash.

Who we rent to

Tourists and visitors with a valid home-country driver's licence, BC residents with a valid Class 5, corporate clients on monthly contracts, and international visitors who can email a passport + licence copy in advance to skip the counter paperwork.

Long-term and business rentals

Rentals longer than 30 days move to a monthly rate that's typically 40–50% below the daily rate, with the option to swap vehicles mid-term. Business clients can set up a corporate account with a single monthly invoice covering all drivers and vehicles. Talk to us before you book if your rental will run longer than a month.

Frequently asked questions new

Do I need a credit card to reserve?
A credit card in the driver's name is required to confirm the booking hold. Debit cards are accepted for final payment at pickup, but not for the hold. If you don't have a credit card, call us before booking and we can usually work something out.
Can I pick up the car outside counter hours?
Yes. Self pick-up is available 24/7 once your booking is confirmed. On the morning of your pickup, you'll receive a text with a lockbox code, a short walkthrough video, and the plate number. Self pickup adds a $25 after-hours processing fee, which we discount to $10 if you book a week or more in advance.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pickup. Inside 48 hours, a one-day rental fee applies. No-shows are charged the full rental amount. For monthly rentals, we ask for 7 days' notice.
Do you deliver to YVR?
We don't deliver to the airport terminal, but our Richmond shop is 10 minutes from YVR and reachable by Canada Line plus a 5-minute walk. If you'd rather not transit with luggage, the taxi from YVR to our shop is a flat $15–20.
What documents do I need at pickup?
Your driver's licence, a second piece of government ID, and the credit card used to book. International visitors should also bring their passport and, if your licence is not in English, an International Driving Permit or a certified translation.
Can I add an additional driver?
Yes. Additional drivers are free as long as they meet our age and licence requirements and are present at pickup so we can add them to the contract. There's no charge for a spouse or a co-worker on a corporate rental.

4 · Sitemap redirect fix background

Right now, cnrcarrental.com/sitemap.xml returns a 301 redirect chain. Google's crawler follows it and lands on /sitemap_index.xml, which is currently returning a 404. The result: Google's guessing which of your pages to crawl, and several of them are being discovered slowly (or not at all).

Here's what I see today when I curl the URL:

$ curl -sI -L https://cnrcarrental.com/sitemap.xml HTTP/2 301 location: https://cnrcarrental.com/sitemap_index.xml x-redirect-by: Rank Math HTTP/2 404 content-type: text/html; charset=UTF-8 page not found.

After the fix, the same command returns:

$ curl -sI -L https://cnrcarrental.com/sitemap.xml HTTP/2 200 content-type: application/xml; charset=UTF-8 HTTP/2 200 content-type: application/xml; charset=UTF-8 (valid sitemap_index.xml, lists every indexable page)

The fix is in Rank Math's sitemap settings (clear the redirect, point it at the right module) plus a hard sitemap_index.xml verification. Once the URL returns 200 application/xml, I submit it to Search Console and Google re-crawls within a day or two.
